SGA Elections

The Elections Committee's goal is to ensure a fair and transparent way for students to choose their representatives for the Student Government Association and class positions. Students who want to run for a position should come to an interest meeting at the start of the semester to find out about available roles.
The SGA general elections take place online during the fall and spring semesters. If there are any empty positions from the spring elections, a special election is held in the second week of the fall semester to fill those spots.


Fall Elections

  • Freshman Class officers
  • Vacant SGA position left from the general elections will be held in the spring.

Spring Elections

  • Student Government Association President
  • Vice President of Internal Affairs
  • Vice President of External Affairs
  • Secretary
  • Attorney General
  • Treasurer
  • Miss A&T
  • Mister A&T
  • Senators
  • Sophomore Class Officers
  • Junior Class Officers
  • Senior Class Officers
  • Student Judiciary Council
  • Elections Committee Members

Submitting a Candidacy Application

Applications can be submitted through 1891Connect.

Creating an 1891 Account

1. Go to1891Connect.
2. To activate your 1891Connect account, you will utilize your OneID information.
3. Once your account has been created, search for Elections Committee.


Access Candidacy Application

4. Search for Elections Committee. (If prompted to join, please join and your request will be granted within 24 hours.)
5. Under Forms search for SGA Election - Candidacy Application.
6. Complete the candidacy Filing Form and Submit.

For questions regarding the election process, please email the Elections Committee at sgaelectionscommittee@ncat.edu.
